Hilton Hotels Timeline

1919

Conrad Hilton opens the first Hilton hotel in Cisco, Texas.

1947

First hotel to install televisions in guest rooms.

1955

Introduced the first multi-hotel loyalty program.

1987

Launch of the Hilton Honors Program.

2002

“Customers Really Matter” (CRM) initiative begins.

2004

Launch of OnQ Technology Platform – the centralized CRM system.

2006

Reacquisition of Hilton International, reunifying the global brand.

2017

Hilton splits into three separate public companies.

2022

Expanded digital key access and contactless check-in.

2024+

Focus on sustainable hospitality and tech-driven personalization.
